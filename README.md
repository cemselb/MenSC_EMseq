**Whole-genome methylation profiling of menstrual stem cells identifies novel biomarkers for endometriosis**

This repository contains scripts used in the bioinformatics analysis of DNA methylation in menstrual blood-derived mesenchymal stem cells (MenSCs), aiming to identify biomarkers related to endometriosis.

**Publication**

Tiniakou, I., Bafligil, C., Pérez-Moraga, R. et al. Whole genome methylation profiling of menstrual stem cells identifies novel biomarkers for endometriosis. Commun Med (2026). https://doi.org/10.1038/s43856-026-01641-3
